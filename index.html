ABOUT US
We are a brother & sister who care about the environment: our air, waters, lands and all their creatures. We are regular people with families and pets, and encourage businesses, individuals, neighborhoods, and governments to show care and concern for the environment. We believe that our efforts, if we join together with all of the great organizations and people out there, could lead to a better and healthier world for us and the people and critters we care about. Greg is a software developer who wants to make a really cool app and Katherine is a volunteer lawyer working solely on this project. Our project will ripen into a charitable organization once we launch our app. You can reach us at getupandgreen@gmail.com.
